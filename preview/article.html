Welcome to ePoster — what to expect

Read
5 min
Updated
May 4, 2026
By
The ePoster team

Keeping a workplace compliant with state and federal labor-law posters used to mean a binder, a quarterly panic, and a few hundred dollars in printing every time something changed. ePoster's job is to make that chore a non-event — the right posters, in the right languages, always up to date, displayed wherever your team actually works.

This article is the orientation for everyone using ePoster for the first time. By the end of it, you'll know what the platform does, who it's for, what to set up first, and where to look when something doesn't go to plan.

What ePoster does

ePoster is a compliance platform for businesses that need to display labor-law posters in their workplace. We track every required federal, state, and city poster in the United States, push updates to your account whenever a regulation changes, and give you several different ways to actually display them — printed, on a TV in the breakroom, embedded on your intranet, or sent to employees by email for a digital sign-off.

You manage everything from a single dashboard. Add a state, remove a state, branch by location, customize with your logo, and let us handle the rest.

Who it's for

ePoster is built for the person responsible for compliance — usually an HR lead, an office manager, or a founder wearing five hats. You don't need to be a lawyer or have a compliance background. The platform makes the right choices on your behalf, and our team is one email away if you get stuck.

Setup at a glance

Most teams are fully set up within ten minutes. The order matters less than getting the basics in: states, displays, sign-offs.

  1. Pick the states you operate in

    Federal posters are added automatically. Add a state and we'll bundle the right state and city posters into your set.

  2. Choose how you'll display them

    Print, breakroom screen, intranet widget, email sign-off — pick one or all four. You can change this any time.

  3. Invite your team

    Add anyone who needs to acknowledge or manage posters. Roles default to "view only" so you decide who can edit.

  4. Turn on update notifications

    We'll email you whenever a poster you display changes. Most months, the answer is "nothing to do."

Our philosophy

We try to make the platform invisible. The best compliance tool is one you forget about, because it just works. That goal shapes every product decision we make — fewer settings, fewer notifications, more trust that the right thing is happening in the background.
